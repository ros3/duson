Disagreements with a fellow student / colleague

If a dispute or concern arises between you and a fellow graduate student or colleague, you may try first to resolve it amicably between yourselves. If informal discussion does not resolve the problem, there are a few avenues to pursue, usually starting with your faculty advisor or supervisor.
