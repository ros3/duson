I want to report an incident of harassment or discrimination to the Office for Institutional Equity.

Action Item

File a report with the Office for Institutional Equity (OIE)

This will be a non-confidential report.

This report will trigger action.

What happens after you file a report (in order):

  1. OIE will contact the involved parties (the person being harassed + the person harassing).
  2. OIE will assign an investigator, who will conduct an investigation.
  3. The appropriate deans in The Graduate School will be notified, and they will reach out to the leadership of your program.
  4. The investigator will conduct interviews with all involved parties, and with witnesses if needed.
  5. The investigator will write a report of findings â€“ the report will get sent to the involved parties.
  6. A report will be archived in OIE. It will also be shared with you (i.e., the person who filed the report) and the appropriate Graduate School leadership.
